Graha Study Notes

Meaning of “Graha”

Graha derives from the root meaning “to seize / eclipse.” A graha has the power to eclipse the mind.

Rahu & Ketu

  • Rahu reveals unquenchable desires; wants to “eat it all.” Blogs, trends, foreign or unusual experiences—including a foreign spouse in marriage charts.
  • Ketu seeks detachment; represents those outside society—monks, nuns, spiritual recluses.
  • Rahu eclipses the Sun; Ketu eclipses the Moon.

Apparent Motion of Planets

  • Always direct: Sun ( Sūrya ) & Moon ( Chandra ).
  • Always retrograde: Rahu & Ketu.
  • All other (Tāra‑graha) planets: direct motion = behave like Sun/Moon; retrograde motion = behave like Rahu/Ketu.
  • Retrograde (vakri) indicates unfinished karma; greater eclipsing power.

In prashna (horary) charts, a retrograde planet ruling the question suggests delays—e.g., a retrograde significator of marriage may postpone marriage for ~1 year or three attempts.

Eight Types of Motion

Five direct  |  Three retrograde:

  • Vakra — standard retrograde
  • Anuvakra — slowing near the end of retrograde
  • Kutila — stationary/turning point

Retrograde planets act as if in the opposite sign; multilateral motion blends both signs.

Outer Planets & Solar Geometry

  • Mars, Jupiter, Saturn station retrograde roughly every 120° from the Sun (Daiva‑koṇa).
  • Sun in 5th house from an outer planet → planet is about to turn retrograde (kutila).
  • Sun in 9th → planet finishing retrograde.
  • Sun opposite (7th) → planet at peak retrograde speed; strongest grahana.
  • Sun in 10th or 4th → planet firmly direct.

Illustrative Chart Concept

Blank chart example: Jupiter in Lagna; Sun in 5th (Jupiter nearly stationary); Saturn opposite Sun (maximum retrograde).

Graha & Rāśi Relationship

  • Rāśi = resources; Graha throws its shadow on those resources.
  • Planets in the same rāśi share resources; same nakshatra = shared ideology, different nakshatra = differing ideology.

Four Types of Lagna

  • 1st house — Udaya Lagna
  • 4th house — Ghaṭī Lagna
  • 7th house — Asta Lagna
  • 10th house — Ākāśa Lagna

Destiny is primarily steered by the Lagna and its lord.

Samyoga & Sambandha

  • Samyoga — any planetary combination.
  • Sambandha — a specific relation (e.g., yuti).

The exact longitude of their meeting is the Samyoga Sphuṭa and can denote:

  • Bhaviṣya‑yoga — future‑fructifying
  • Atīta‑yoga — past / separated
  • Viyoga — separation event

Samukha Rāśi refers to the compensatory adjustment of signs sharing resources.
