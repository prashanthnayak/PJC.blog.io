Astrological Concepts Overview

Lagna (Ascendants) and Their Significations

LagnaPhasePrimary Signification
Udaya LagnaSunriseBuddhi – Beginning of day/life
Asta LagnaSunsetVriddhi – Growth as sun sets and moon rises
Svarga / Aakash LagnaMid-heavenSiddhi – Full potential of the Sun
Jala LagnaMid-nightSiddhi – Full potential of the Moon

These four lagnas are considered Vishnu sthanas—they sustain life.
