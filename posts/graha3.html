Ṣaḍ-ripu — The Six Human Vices

  1. Kāma – Lust
  2. Krodha – Anger
  3. Lobha – Greed
  4. Madā – Arrogance
  5. Moha – Attachment
  6. Mātsarya – Jealousy

Pañca-makāra

  • Madya – Alcohol
  • Māṁsa – Flesh
  • Matsya (also rendered Marsala) – Fish
  • Maithuna – Sexual union
  • Mudrā – Esoteric gesture/posture

Digpala

  • Ketu in Digpala — upward direction; Ananta deviate
  • Ketu signifies high-rise buildings, hilly areas, centre of the country
  • Lagna — downward motion
  • Digpala from Lagna — Devata giving knowledge to Lagna
  • Kendra to Arudha Lagna — blessings in the physical direction

Graha — Devata — Blessings

  • Surya — Indra: full oversight; control over things
  • Chandra — Vayu: makes things happen fast; strength of breath like Hanuman
  • Mangal — Yama: knowledge about death, longevity, borders, antaka
  • Budha — Kubera: knowledge about wealth
  • Guru — Isana: complete knowledge of reasoning and power
  • Shukra — Agni: visual power, design, creativity
  • Shani — Varuna: knowledge of labor; can carry huge burdens
  • Rahu — Nirriti: giver of disorder; can also remove it
  • Ketu — Ananta: meditation
  • Lagna — Brahma: longevity

Additional Principles

  • Planets conjoined receive the combined blessings of their respective Devatas.
  • Go (or face) in the direction ruled by a planet that has Digpala blessing.
  • Disposition can lock or unlock a planet's blessing.
  • When a planet is debilitated, seek guidance from the corresponding Mahavidya.
  • Planetary combustion signifies the anger of Shiva.
  • Example chart to study: Abraham Lincoln.
  • Rahu + Mangal combination — Kuja Stambhana.
