Nakṣatra Information Portal

There are 27 Nakṣatras. The 28th scheme includes Abhijit.

Key Concepts

  • 1. The Moon lords the mind, and its Nakṣatra is used to see how the mind is disposed.
  • 2. Abhijit spans from 6:40 – 10:54:13 in Capricorn. It is known as The Victorious One (Hari).
  • 3. Abhijit cannot be considered a Jana Nakṣatra as it does not have four complete pādas (quarters).

Nakṣatra Pāda (Feet)

Each Nakṣatra has 4 pāda or feet. These four pādas correspond to the four goals of life:

  • 1st pada: Dharma – duty (trine to the 1st house)
  • 2nd pada: Artha – wealth (trine to the 10th house)
  • 3rd pada: Kāma – desire (trine to the 7th house)
  • 4th pada: Mokṣa – liberation (trine to the 4th house)

Navāmśa Mapping by Pada

  • 1st pada → Aries, Leo, Sagittarius
  • 2nd pada → Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn
  • 3rd pada → Gemini, Libra, Aquarius
  • 4th pada → Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces
