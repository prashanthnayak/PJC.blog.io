Rashi Drishti

Rashi share mutual information

Graham drishti is the desire of a graham to use the information that might influence the decision making process.

Abhimukha Rashi and Parsva Rashi

Planets in a rashi can help or cause problems to its abhimukha rashi.

Example: Rahu in Makara can cause problems to Simha rashi

Retrograde planets, if benefic, will add to the stubbornness of protection.

Parsva Rashi Classifications

  • Parsva-left: Knowledge
  • Parsva-right: Destruction

Note: In chart for Simha, parsva is reversed
