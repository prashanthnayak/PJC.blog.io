Panchanga

Introduction

Panchanga (sometimes written “Panchangs”) describes the quality of time at birth. It forms the foundation for interpreting many other factors in a horoscope, and in certain cases its combinations can even overrule prominent yogas in the natal chart.

House Associations

When judging a chart, consider each limb from a corresponding house:

  • Vara – viewed from the Lagna (1st house).
  • Tithi – viewed from the 4th house.
  • Nakshatra – viewed from the 7th house (Janma Nakshatra).
  • Karana – viewed from the 10th house.
