Rashi Symbol and Flaws

All the rashi names indicate the names of symbols. Rashi are advaita or dvaita. Dvaita rashis (dual or multiple elements) carry Brahma dosha.

Advaita Rashis

  • Mesha
  • Vrsha
  • Karka (Cancer)
  • Simha

Planetary Guna Types

  • Satva: Guru, Chandra, Surya
  • Rajas: Budha, Shukra
  • Tamas: Saturn, Mars

Example: Mesha is a Rajas rashi, but its lord Mars is Tamas, so it combines Rajas + Tamas. Kanya has Satva (rashi) + Rajas (Mercury) + Tamas (Rahu). Dominance depends on strongest planet.

Purusha–Stri, Krura–Akrura Classification

Krura (Male Signs, Odd)

  • Mesha, Mithuna, Simha, Tula, Dhanu, Kumbha

Akrura (Female Signs, Even)

  • Vrsha, Karka, Kanya, Ali, Makara, Meena

Note: This classification should not be used for determining a child's gender. Variations exist, e.g., Mithuna and Karka exchange, Kumbha and Meena swap.
