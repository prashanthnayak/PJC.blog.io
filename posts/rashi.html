Graham Sadhana and Bhava Viveka

Key Concepts

  • Bhava Chakra — Add 15° on both sides of the Lagna. This creates a new chart where the Lagna remains unchanged, but the planetary positions can shift.
  • Bhava Chalit Chakra — Uses the Bhava Lagna (a special Lagna) by taking 15° before and after the Lagna.
  • When multiple planets change houses, major life shifts tend to occur.
  • Bhava Chalit Chakra is considered our biological clock. Start at 6 a.m., where the Sun is positioned, and continue plotting the rest of the chart.

Tip: Try constructing the Bhava Chalit Chakra with your own birth chart to observe how these adjustments alter house positions and potential interpretations.
