Graham Drishti

Key Points

  • An expression of desire
  • Ability to influence other bodies stationed at certain positions
  • Awareness—knowing and viewing something

Planetary Drishti Types

Planet Houses Aspected Drishti Name
Saturn 3rd & 10th Upachaya Drishti
Jupiter 5th & 9th Trikona Drishti
Mars 4th & 8th Chatursaya Drishti
Sun, Moon, Venus, Mercury 7th Kama Drishti

Drishti from Karaka (Examples)

  • Protection to marriage: 4th & 8th from the 7th lord
  • Protection to job: 4th & 8th from the 10th lord
  • Protection to wealth: 4th & 8th from the 2nd house

Interpretative Notes

Work (3rd & 10th Houses)

  • 3rd house – new initiatives or work
  • 10th house – established work or unresolved responsibilities
  • 3rd from 7th lord – new growth in relationships
  • 10th from 7th lord – lingering issues in relationships

Knowledge (5th & 9th Houses)

  • 5th house – new knowledge, intuition, creativity
  • 9th house – knowledge received from guru or higher learning
